# How To Onboard A Shop

## 1. Create Owner Account

Ask the owner to install the APK and sign in with Google.

## 2. Create Shop

Tap Create, enter the shop name, and save.

## 3. Print QR

Open the shop dashboard, tap QR registration, and copy or share the registration link/QR. Print it near billing.

## 4. Register First Customer

Scan the QR with a phone. Enter name, WhatsApp number, tick consent, and submit.

## 5. Connect WhatsApp

Open WhatsApp in the app, connect or reconnect, and scan the Evolution API QR if prompted.

## 6. Send Festival Greeting

Open Campaigns, choose a festival, edit the message, and send.
